Bethany Heck

Desktop

  1. The adjectives I would describe her profile with would be creative, neat, clean, and clear.
  2. The design of the navigation is pretty creative. I really like how on the left side of her page she has the angel halo and the devil horns. I thought that was pretty cute since her last name is Heck. The pages she has on her profile are her about, her work, and her blog.
  3. The design of the introductory information is bold and in your face. It really pops out at you and is different. What I've learned about Bethany is that she is playful and likes to have fun, but she is serious about her work.
  4. The design and layout of the body content is nice and ordered so it looks easy to navigate the website.
  5. The design of the footer is soft but noticable and fun.The information she includes here are her social media links and other websites.

Mobile

  1. The navigation has changed by getting smaller and on the top right. The navigation has changed into three horizontal lines.
  2. The size of the headline text has changed by getting a little bit smaller and taking up more of the page than before.
  3. The size of the body text has changed by appearing bigger than before and it looks more smooshed together.
  4. The layout of the content changed by there being less on the page that you can see at one time.
  5. The layout of the footer changed by you not being able to see it right away, at least until you scroll down. The footer images are now stacked on top of one another.

Olivia Tytherleigh

Desktop

  1. The adjectives I would use to describe Olivia's portfolio would be bold, bright, colorful and in your face.
  2. The layout and design of the introductory section is shown using bright colors and lots of pictures that break up space in between everything. Olivia indicates that you're about to see a new portfolio entry by changing the background color around it. Olivia lays out the product description by using bold, white text that catches your eye. Olivia lays out the project images by using lots of color and noticibly separating images.
  3. The layout and design of the footer is kind of simple, but it works and is easy to see.

Mobile

  1. The layout of the introductory sections chanes by the header image disapearing.
  2. The layout of the portfolio entries change by showing you less of what it did before. Olivia indicates that you're going to see a new portfolio entry by a white line that divides the page. Olivia lays out the project description by a different color background. Olivia lays out the project images by separating them by a white space around them.
